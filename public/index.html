Have questions? We have answers

Frequently asked questions

How long does delivery take inside Accra?

For standard orders placed before 10:00 AM, we offer same-day delivery to your home, office or shop. Orders after 10:00 AM are scheduled for the next morning. Custom event bundles need 48 hours' notice for cold-chain planning.

How can I track my order?

Every order gets a reference code like JF-20260711-XXXXXX when you check out. Keep it handy — our team will confirm your delivery window by phone.

What payment methods do you accept?

Mobile Money, direct bank transfer, and cash on delivery. Bulk and wholesale buyers receive a formatted invoice.

Are the juices natural, and how long do they keep?

Yes — 100% natural with no added sweeteners, colourings or chemical preservatives. Keep refrigerated and consume within the printed shelf life for best taste.

Do you offer special rates for shops, gyms and schools?

Absolutely. Bulk orders unlock tiered per-bottle pricing automatically once you reach a product's minimum quantity. Reach out for recurring weekly retainer rates.
